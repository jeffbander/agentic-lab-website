## About the Agentic Laboratory

The Agentic Laboratory is pioneering a new model for healthcare technology development -- one where clinicians, not just engineers, build production-grade software using multi-agent AI systems. Operating under the Mount Sinai Health System, we have demonstrated that physician-developers, working with orchestrated AI agents, can deliver secure, HIPAA-compliant applications in weeks rather than the months or years traditional development cycles require.

Our results speak to the significance of this work:

- **5+ production applications** deployed within a major health system
- **92%+ AI-assisted code generation** with rigorous human-in-the-loop oversight
- **$1.5M+ in documented cost savings** across projects
- **2-6 week deployment cycles** for clinical-grade software

We believe we are at an inflection point in healthcare technology. The convergence of large language models, multi-agent orchestration frameworks, and clinician domain expertise is creating an unprecedented opportunity to fundamentally reimagine how healthcare software is conceived, built, and maintained. The implications extend well beyond our laboratory -- this model has the potential to transform how every hospital system, research institution, and healthcare organization approaches technology development.
